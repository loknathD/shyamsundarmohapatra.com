Dr. Shyamsundar Mohapatra

Dr. Shyamsundar Mohapatra International Foundation

Dr. Shyamsundar Mohapatra former member of parliament (Loksabha and Rajyasabha) was born in the year 1929 at Nachinta,Khantapada, Odisha. His Father name was Shree Narendranath Mohapatra and mothers name was Nishamani Devi, He had two brothers namely, Shree Pitambar Mohapatra and Sudhakar Mohapatra and one Sister namely Shantimayee Devi.
He was married to Dr. Radha Devi and had two sons namely Shree Subhankar Mohapatra and Dr. Jyoti Mohapatra and one daughter namely Minati Mohapatra.
Grand Children are Dr. Ashavaree Das, Mr. Sanak Mohapatra, Meghna manasweeni Mohapatra and Manish Harshavardhan Mohapatra.
Daughter in laws are Mrs. Mamata Mohapatra and Mrs. Gopamudra Mohapatra.
