The Final Journey...

OMM SHANTI

Dr. S.S Mohapatra’s ashes has been immersed in river Ganga and all religious rivers and Puri sea in India.

Simultaneously his ashes has been immersed in river Han in South Korea, River Thames in London, River Hudson in New York and most notably his ashes has been immersed at the pond at Bongeunsa, Buddhist Temple in South Korea near graduate institute of peace studies (Kyung Hee Unversity) where he used to teach.

Dr. Jyoti Mohapatra and Dr. Hong ki kim, Dear friend of Dr. S.S Mohaptra among other friends immersing the ashes of Dr. S.S Mohaptra at the pond of Bongeunsa temple (Buddhist temple) near seoul, South Korea.
